Our mission is to create an application that enables our users to track their daily emotions so that they have a tool to measure their emotional well being and mental health during the day.

Mental health includes our emotional, psychological, and social well-being. It affects how we think, feel, and act. It also helps determine how we handle stress, relate to others, and make choices. Mental health is important at every stage of life, from childhood and adolescence through adulthood. By using this app, you are increasing your ability to become more emotionally aware, make better decisions, and become a better communicator.
